After the Beep

Esaadi's involvement in Arts and Cultural settings includes his contribution to the 'After the Beep' exhibition (2021). Curated by Sarah Daher and Anna Bernice, and presented at Satellite Gallery in Dubai, Esaadi created a textile-based outcome in response to artwork created by fellow emerging and established artists in the region, inspired by the 'telephone' game.
