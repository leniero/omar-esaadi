Divas of Arabia

Divas of Arabia (2022) served as Esaadi's dissertation project during his tenure at Chelsea College of Arts. Inspired by the legacy and creative and political impact of female artists in the Pan-Arab region, Esaadi responded with a series of textiles and mixed-media outcomes, outcomes realised with 3D modelling and printing, as well as a written dissertation critically assessing historic and current female artists in the region.
