Johanna Parv

Esaadi served as a brand coordinator and studio manager to Johanna Parv for seasons SS25 and AW25, as well as additional support with seasons AW24 and SS26. his range of responsibilities under Parv included overseeing sales outreach to buyers and clients, production supervision including Quality Checks, assembly of Bill of Materials, overseeing progress with factories, materials sourcing and assisting with tech packs and pattern amendments. His creative input to the brand also included support in the assembly of the AW25 Paris showroom, assistance on campaign shoots for AW25 and SS26, and oversaw the materialisation of a custom made instillation for Dover Street Market: Ginza as part of their AW24 open day event.
