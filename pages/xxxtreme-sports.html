XXXtremeSports

XXXtremeSports is a fashion label founded and designed by Omar Esaadi. Developed as his graduate collection in 2023, the collection is an accumulation of Esaadi's interests, aesthetics and philosophies into a ready to wear proposal. Through the lens of sportswear and customised textiles, Esaadi communicates larger narratives around culture, identity, community and self expression. Since its conception, the collection has been recognized by a range of platforms such as 3EIB (a London-based grassroots collective for Arab designers and creatives), DAZED, The Rage Mag and recognised as a runner up for the Institute of Digital Fashion and Oakley's 'Future 5' project. @xtremesportsxx
