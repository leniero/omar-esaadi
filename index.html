Omar Esaadi is a detail-oriented, multi-hyphenate creative working across design and administrative based projects for fashion, arts and cultural industries. Esaadi has spent five years across his education and industrial experience supporting independent talent and larger organisations across a range of needs such as design, sales, marketing, production and logistics. He is also the founder and creative director of his fashion label, XXXtremeSports, which has been recognised by platforms such as 3EIB, DAZED, The Rage Mag and the Institute of Digital Fashion. Esaadi positions himself as a competent, collaborative and driven individual with a strong awareness of current markets, trends and interests of a range of demographics, as well as a niche ability to create a hybrid between creative, conceptual ideations and pragmatic, tangible outcomes.
